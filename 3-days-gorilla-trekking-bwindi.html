Day One: Destination Journey

The Journey begins from either Kampala City or Entebbe airport and may take between 7-9 hours. The Journey itself may appear long but trust me; you are likely to even lengthen it because of the amazing attractions that lie along the route. Starting from a brief view of the Capital city you will hit the road and before long, you are at the equator where you can briefly stop over for a photo a moment of relaxation. Resuming the journey you will encounter the vast lands of central Uganda, different cultural souvenirs, pass through the outskirts of lake Mburo National Park, Igongo cultural Museum before reaching Mbarara city for a refreshing Lunch. • Hit the last section of the Journey from mbarara and spent the Night in Kabale town, or head straight to bwindi depending on the time. A range of lodges is available for this so no worries, these will always be communicated in time. Places like Rushaga Havens Lodge, Trekkers Tavern lodge, etc. • Once on this section of the tour, Lunch and dinner will be provided plus drinking water, extras can always be provided privately.

Day Two: The tour

The day is gone, you are now in the southwestern part of Uganda, waking up to a cool morning, Early breakfast already served on the table. Warm ypourself up for the day of your memories. From breakfast you will be driven to Rhiija park headquarters for a briefing on the whole exercise. This will end in the trek into the jungles. Depending on the location of the families of these Gorillas, the journey can take you from 1-6 hours. Once they are found, you will be given one hour to spend with them observing their manners, this as stipulated by the Wild Life Authority. In this hour you can take photos, and any other friendly interactions and from this, w shall return to the headquarters where you will get the certificate as confirmation that you have successfully conducted this gorilla tour. From this, we return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day Three: Return to the Airport or Kampala or Entebbe or to the Next internal tour destination.

This day begins with breakfast as usual and after this you will be driven to your next destination for your post tour activities. This can be the airport or another destination that you would have chosen. We definitely have other offers apart from this package, fell free to browse through and we shall readily serve you.
