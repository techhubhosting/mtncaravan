Description:

This opportunity presents you a rare opportunity to get into the best tour destinations of Uganda in a relatively small period of time. Key Points

In these 10 Days you will get the finest destinations Uganda has to offer from Entebbe Airport to Lake Mburo, home to 300 Plus bird species as well as zebra, giraffe, buffalo etc, Then proceed to Bwindi for the one in a lifetime gorilla trekking experience.
