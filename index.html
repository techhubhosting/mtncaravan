At Mountain Gorilla Caravan Safaris, we have one focus, and that is simply to make your African safari the experience of a lifetime without breaking the bank. To create that unique and authentic safari experience, we brought together expert local guides, like-minded fun-spirited travellers, premium accommodation, incredible prices, and wonderful destinations from exotic places in two currently popular travel countries, Uganda and Rwanda, such as Bwindi Impenetrable Forest, Volcanoes National Park, Murchison Falls, Kibale Forest, Queen Elizabeth National Park, Kampala City or Kigali City, to the adrenaline capital Jinja the source of the mighty Nile river and the restless culturs of the two countries, night life, dance, food or the sounds of the authoritative Silverback mountain gorilla.
