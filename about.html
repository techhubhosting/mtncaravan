MOUNTAIN GORILLA CARAVAN SAFARIS

Our safari itineraries and excursions have been designed keeping our distinguished customers in mind, and developed through personal experience. We understand that each customer has a different expectation from a safari, and so while some prefer a thrilling adventure, others tend towards a quieter, more relaxed time Still others look for a romantic getaway or just great family time. Each of our itinerary packages therefore is tailored to meet the expectations of our guests, but each has one objective in common. To make the safari experience, memorable and something to be cherished forever.

How we create your Journey

1. Speak to a specialist

Get inspired by the safari ideas on our website. Then speak to us for first-hand safari expert advice and if you're ready, start planning your Uganda safari in detail.

2. Plan and refine your safari

Your safari specialist will carefully design your safari into a detailed trip itinerary. We'll keep perfecting your itinerary until you're completely happy and ready to book, at which point you pay your deposit.

3. Help as you prepare

After you've booked, you can call your safari specialist as and when with any last questions about your safari trip. We'll go through your itinerary with you a week before you travel.
